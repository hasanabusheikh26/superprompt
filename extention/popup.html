SuperPrompt Help

How to Use SuperPrompt

SuperPrompt works just like Grammarly, but for AI prompts:

  • Highlight any text in input fields, text areas, or chat boxes
  • Click the floating ‚ö° icon that appears
  • Review the enhanced prompt and choose to copy or replace
  • Your enhancement history is saved automatically

Keyboard Shortcuts

Speed up your workflow with these shortcuts:

  • Ctrl/‚åò + Shift + P - Open SuperPrompt popup
  • Ctrl/‚åò + Shift + E - Enhance selected text
  • Escape - Close any SuperPrompt dialog

Supported Websites

SuperPrompt works on all websites with text inputs, including:

  • ChatGPT, Claude, and other AI chatbots
  • Gmail, Outlook, and email clients
  • Social media platforms (Twitter, LinkedIn, etc.)
  • Google Docs, Notion, and writing tools
  • Any website with text input fields

Privacy & Security

Your privacy is important to us:

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Only selected text is sent for enhancement
  • No browsing history or personal data is collected
  • You can clear your history anytime
